Drop hero background images here (JPG or PNG).

Guidance for a hero background:
- Wide landscape orientation (the hero is full-width).
- High resolution: at least ~1920px wide. Bigger is fine; the build
  auto-resizes to 1600px wide and compresses, so you don't need to
  shrink them yourself.
- Prefer a real, premium building photo (ideally a property you manage).
  Strong, clean lighting works best since a dark overlay sits on top so
  the headline and form stay readable.

After uploading, tell Claude the filename(s) and the best one will be
wired in as the hero background. Multiple files = candidates to compare.
